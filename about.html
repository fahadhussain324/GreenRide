About GreenRide

GreenRide was founded in 2018 to transform urban commuting through electric, low-emission transport options

The company began with a small fleet of 20 e-bikes and has since expanded to over 6,000 vehicles across major UK cities.

GreenRide partners with local councils, sustainability groups, and universities to promote safe and eco-friendly travel.
