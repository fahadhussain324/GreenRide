Understanding Our Plans

Basic Plan

Perfect for Casual Riders

The Basic Plan is ideal for casual users who want to enjoy the benefits of eco-friendly transportation without a long-term commitment. With 60 minutes of free rides per month, you can explore your city while making a positive environmental impact. This plan is perfect for those taking an occasional trip or exploring the GreenRide experience.

What's Included:

  • 60 minutes of free rides/month
  • Access to all standard vehicles
  • Discounted rates after free minutes
  • 24/7 customer support
Best For: Occasional riders and explorers

For Regular Commuters

The Plus Plan is designed for regular commuters who need reliable daily transportation. Whether you're heading to work, school, or weekend adventures, the Plus Plan has you covered.

  • Priority customer support
